       Apps and Websites

JP Dictionary

This app helps users translate any word from English to Japanese and vice versa.

The fully customizable lists let users add new lists, rename them, and even filter them to easily find a list.🌲.

Users can add words to their chosen list by clicking the plus button.

They can type Romaji, English, Hiragana/Katakana, or Kanji in the search bar to find any Japanese or Engligh equivalent.

The Japanese or English parts can be hidden to aid memorization

Dark mode reduces eye strain for users.

Sous Chef

This app displays random recipes but also lets users search for any recipe from an extensive database of all cuisines.

The user can click on a recipe to see the details like ingredients, cook time, and servings. Clicking the play button displays the instructions for the recipe step by step.

The user can scroll through the steps by hand or they can activate voice recognition to be hands free while cooking. The voice commands 'next', 'back', and 'exit' can be used to easily navigate through the recipe.
